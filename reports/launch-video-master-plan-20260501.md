## 1) What CampSite Is (Detailed Explanation)

CampSite is an **execution and people-operations platform** built to help teams run high-accountability work without losing context.

In most companies, execution data gets fragmented:

- hiring updates in one tool
- HR operations in another
- manager notes in docs
- status in chat
- decisions in meetings that are never captured properly

CampSite unifies these into one operating layer so teams can move fast with shared reality.

### Core problem CampSite solves

Teams do not usually fail due to lack of talent. They fail because:

- context is scattered
- ownership is ambiguous
- progress is hard to trust
- decisions are disconnected from execution
- leaders get stale snapshots instead of live operational clarity

CampSite addresses this by turning fragmented operational work into structured, visible, actionable workflows.

### What CampSite covers

CampSite supports operational workflows across:

- recruitment and hiring pipelines
- HR onboarding and employee process flows
- one-on-ones and performance cycle coordination
- manager and admin oversight
- internal broadcasts and organizational updates
- profile and team context views

### How CampSite works (operational model)

1. **Structured intake:** requests, actions, and updates enter a clear workflow shape.
2. **Ownership by default:** every item has a clear owner, stage, and next step.
3. **Role-aware visibility:** HR, managers, admins, and team members see the right level of detail.
4. **Execution-linked context:** decisions remain attached to real work, not scattered in side channels.
5. **Continuous iteration:** teams can tighten workflows without introducing new process overhead.

### Why this matters

CampSite reduces operational drag and meeting-heavy alignment loops. The goal is not cosmetic dashboards; the goal is **faster, clearer execution under real-world team complexity**.

### Positioning line options

- **Primary:** CampSite is the execution system for modern teams.
- **Alternative:** CampSite keeps decisions, ownership, and execution in one place.
- **People-ops angle:** CampSite connects hiring, HR, and team execution into one operating layer.

## 6) Camera Angles + Shot Architecture

## A-roll angles

- **A1:** medium close-up, eye-level, slight off-center composition
- **A2:** tighter close-up for emotionally important lines
- **A3:** two-shot medium-wide for shared statements

## B-roll categories

- product interaction shots
- workflow and collaboration inserts
- city atmosphere and scale shots
- detail macro shots (hands, keyboard, reflections)

## Coverage rule for each narrative beat

For each beat, capture:

- one primary speaking shot
- one product visual
- one contextual b-roll shot
- one alternate angle

This gives maximum edit flexibility and avoids rigid assembly.
